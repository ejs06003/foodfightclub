# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

Food Fight Club is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use Food Fight Club, we collect the following information:

- **Username:** Your chosen display name for the game  
- **Game Statistics:** Points, achievements, leaderboard position, throw history  
- **Device Information:** Device type, iOS version, app version  
- **Game Progress:** Inventory items, unlocked cosmetics, daily challenges  
- **In-App Purchase History:** Records of cosmetic items purchased  

We do **NOT** collect:

- Your real name or contact information (unless you contact support)  
- Location data  
- Photos or camera access  
- Contacts or calendar information  
- Any personally identifiable information beyond your username  

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the Food Fight Club experience:

- **Game Progress:** Synchronize your game data and enable cloud-based features  
- **Leaderboards:** Display rankings and competitive features  
- **Achievements:** Track and reward your accomplishments  
- **In-App Purchases:** Process and restore cosmetic item purchases  
- **Game Balance:** Analyze gameplay data to improve game mechanics  
- **Real-time Features:** Enable multiplayer interactions and notifications  
- **Technical Support:** Assist you if you contact us with issues  

We will **NEVER**:

- Sell your data to third parties  
- Use your data for advertising  
- Share your information with other players beyond your chosen username  
- Track you across other apps or websites  

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored securely using Supabase, a secure cloud database platform:

- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL  
- Data is encrypted at rest in secure data centers  
- Database access is protected by Row Level Security policies  
- Authentication is handled securely via Apple Sign In  
- We implement industry-standard security measures  
- Local data is stored on your device using iOS security protections  
- We take reasonable measures to protect your data  

### Data Retention

- Active account data is retained while you use the app  
- Deleted accounts and their data are permanently removed within 30 days  
- Aggregated, non-identifiable gameplay data may be retained to improve game balance  

While we take security seriously, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you of any known breaches.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We share minimal data as necessary to operate the game.

### Public information (visible to other players):

- Your username  
- Your leaderboard position and points  
- Your throw history with other players  
- Your selected character skin  

### Information NOT shared with other players:

- Device information  
- In-app purchase history  
- Detailed gameplay statistics  
- Any personal information  

### Third-party sharing:

- **Supabase:** Stores your game data to enable cloud features (see Section 7)  
- **Apple:** Processes in-app purchases (we never see payment information)  
- No other third parties receive your data  

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to anyone.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access:** View all your game data within the app  
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings  
- **Opt-Out:** Disable notifications, sounds, and haptics in Settings  
- **Data Portability:** Contact us to request an export of your data in machine-readable format  
- **Correction:** Update your username and game preferences anytime  

### To delete your account:

1. Go to **Settings > Account**  
2. Tap **Delete Account**  
3. Confirm deletion  

This will permanently delete all your game data from our servers and your device within 30 days. In-app purchase records are retained by Apple for billing purposes per their policy.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Food Fight Club complies with children’s privacy laws, including COPPA:

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children  
- No real names, addresses, or contact information are required  
- No social media integration or chat features  
- In-app purchases are cosmetic only and require parental approval via the App Store  
- No third-party advertising or tracking  

Parents: If you believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 7. Third-Party Services

Food Fight Club uses the following services:

### Apple Services

- Apple Sign In: Secure authentication (covered by Apple’s Privacy Policy)  
- In-App Purchases: Processed by Apple (covered by Apple’s Privacy Policy)  
- Push Notifications: Delivered by Apple (covered by Apple’s Privacy Policy)  

### Third-Party Services

- **Supabase:** Cloud database and backend services  
  - Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy  
  - Stores game data, user profiles, and gameplay statistics  
  - Provides real-time features and data synchronization  
  - Data is hosted in secure, compliant data centers  
  - Subject to Supabase’s security and privacy standards  

We do not use any third-party advertising or tracking services.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app with an updated **Last Updated** date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Significant changes will be highlighted in the app and may require your acknowledgment.

---

## 9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

**Email:** support@foodfightclub.com  

We will respond to all inquiries within 30 days.
